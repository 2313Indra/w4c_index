Frequently Asked Questions

How can my city use the W4C Index?

The W4C Index provides a comprehensive framework for assessing your city's water sensitivity. City administrators can use it to benchmark performance, identify areas for improvement, and track progress towards water-sensitive goals.

What kind of data is needed?

The index uses a hybrid approach, combining quantitative data (for Technology, Environment, and Society realms) with qualitative assessments (for Governance). Data requirements are flexible and can be adapted to your city's available resources.

How long does an assessment take?

The duration varies based on data availability and city size. A complete assessment typically takes 2-3 months, but cities can start with a phased approach focusing on key indicators.

Is there support available for implementation?

Yes, we provide guidance on the assessment process, data requirements, and implementation strategies. Our team can assist with capacity building and technical support throughout the process.
